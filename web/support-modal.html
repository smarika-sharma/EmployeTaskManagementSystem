Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I create a new task?

+

To create a new task, navigate to the Tasks section and click the "Add New Task" button. Fill in the required fields including title, description, due date, and assignee. Click "Save" to create the task.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

+

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive the email immediately.

I can't log into my account. What should I do?

+

First, make sure you're using the correct email address and password. If you're still having trouble, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If the issue persists, contact our support team with your email address and we'll help you regain access to your account.

How do I change my password?

+

To change your password, go to your Profile page and click on "Change Password" in the Password Management section. Enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm. Make sure your new password meets the security requirements (at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers).

Can I assign tasks to multiple team members?

+

Yes, you can assign tasks to multiple team members. When creating or editing a task, you can select multiple users from the assignee dropdown. Each assigned member will receive notifications about the task.

How do I set task priorities?

+

Task priorities can be set when creating or editing a task. Choose from Low, Medium, High, or Urgent priority levels. Priority affects how tasks are displayed and sorted in your task list.

How do I update my profile information?

+

Go to your Profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can then modify your personal information. Note that some fields like Employee ID and Role may be restricted and can only be changed by an administrator.

I'm not receiving email notifications. How can I fix this?

+

Check your email settings in your Profile page. Make sure your email address is correct and that you haven't accidentally unsubscribed from notifications. Also check your spam folder. If the issue continues, contact support to verify your email preferences.

How do I mark a task as completed?

+

To mark a task as completed, go to your task list and click on the task. Look for the status dropdown and change it from "In Progress" or "Pending" to "Completed". You can also add comments or notes before marking it complete.

Is there a mobile app available?

+

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store. The mobile app provides full functionality for task management on the go.

How do I export my task data?

+

To export your task data, go to the Reports section and select "Export Data". You can choose to export in CSV, Excel, or PDF format. Select the date range and data types you want to include in the export.

What should I do if I'm locked out of my account?

+

If you're locked out due to too many failed login attempts, wait 15 minutes and try again. If you're still locked out, use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password. For persistent issues, contact our support team immediately.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

+

If you encounter a bug or technical issue, please contact our support team via email or phone. Include details about what you were doing when the issue occurred, your browser/device information, and any error messages you received.
